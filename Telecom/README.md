<h2>Telecom Churn Prediction using Logistic Regression</h2>

<h3>Overview</h3>
<p>
Telecom churn prediction is a binary classification problem that identifies customers who are likely to discontinue a telecom service. This project applies Logistic Regression, a supervised machine learning algorithm, to analyze customer attributes such as <b>age</b>, <b>tenure</b> and <b>monthly charges</b>.
The model estimates the probability of customer churn and classifies customers
into churn or non-churn categories. Data preprocessing, feature encoding,
handling missing values improve prediction performance.The resulting model enables telecom providers to identify at-risk customers early and take preventive actions.
</p>

<h3>Applications</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Identifies customers who are likely to discontinue telecom services.</li>
    <li>Supports customer retention by enabling targeted offers and loyalty programs.
    <li>Helps reduce customer churn, improving overall business profitability.</li>
    <li>Assists marketing teams in designing personalized promotional campaigns.</li>
    <li>Improves customer satisfaction by addressing potential issues before customers leave.</li>
</ul>
